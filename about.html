About

The Intra Clinic was originally started by Markus "Notch" Persson and even predates Mojang by a year or two. The old website was operational from around 2001-2006. It served as a place for Markus to share his music and games from. Ever since it went offline, The Intra Clinic's domain has been up for grabs. Now, it has a new lease on life.
