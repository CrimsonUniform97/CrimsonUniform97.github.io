Version 1 (25th May 2008; 1:24 am)
The first prototype of the Chambered engine was shared by Notch on May 25th 2008 on the jvm-gaming forums. The game already ran in 320x240 pixels and was scaled 2x for better playability. Full mouse picking support was added, alongside walls, floor and other sprites - including a non-final enemies.

Version 2 (25th May 2008; 2:01 pm)
Additional tile types, mouse look support and a sky in the woods.
