Known issues

  • It doesn't work in netscape (Fixed in 0.2.0)

    Or was it just mozilla? Anyway, this is a known bug with how netscape/mozilla handles applets in jar-files. This will be fixed if I someday find it worthwile to write work-around for other people's bugs.

  • Sometimes items will seem to dissapear (Fixed in 0.2.0)

    This happens VERY rarely and has to do with how I repaint the applet. I only repaint the areas that need to be repainted, but sometimes items don't get drawn as a result of some strange chase-case I haven't found yet. Since just the graphics for the item is missing, one solution is to open and leave a backpack or character equipment-screen.
