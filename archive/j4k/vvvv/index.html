Description

Disaster! While traveling to the beach planet of Relaxia, you and all your crew members suddenly got teleported to a dangerous but strangely familiar place.

Rescue the six stranded crewmen so you can finally bask in both glory and sunlight on Relaxia.

VVVV is a 4K tribute to Terry Cavanagh\'s brilliant game VVVVVV. See http://www.thelettervsixtim.es/ for more details on the original.
This re/demake was make with permission.

Instructions

Move your character with the arrows keys.
Flip gravity with up, space or \'V\'. You can\'t flip gravity while in the air.
Press \'M\' to open a mini map.

Spikes will kill you. Everything else is safe to touch.

Can you reveal the entire map?
Can you beat the game in less than 100 seconds? Can you beat it with less than 10 deaths?
