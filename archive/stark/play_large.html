Instructions:

    To focus the applet so the keys work, simply click on it. If this fails, click anywhere outside the applet first, then click on it again.
    Use the keys described below to move around E1M6, and simply close the browser window when you get bored.

Controls:

    Move forward: Up or W
    Move backward: Down or S
    Strafe left: A
    Strafe right: D
    Turn left: Left
    Turn right: Right
