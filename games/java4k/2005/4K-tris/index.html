4K-tris (http://sweb.cz/petrblahos/4ktris/)
By pb33
Runnable JAR
This game was submitted in the Java4K 2005 Competition. 		Download

Description

Now this is a great 4K version of tetris! The movement is smooth, there's a semi-animated background, and classic gameplay that seems to be flawless. There's also different levels of difficulty!

Instructions

Classic controls: left/right arrows to move, up arrow to rotate, and down arrow to drop.

Installing

Download the JAR and double click it.
